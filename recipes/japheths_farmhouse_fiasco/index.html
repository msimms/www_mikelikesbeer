Japheth's Farmhouse Fiasco

Description

This receipe is for an all-grain, small batch Peppercorn Saison (sometimes described as a farmhouse ale) with an optional mango addition. Saisons are a popular Belgian style

To make it a mango peppercorn saison, I added crystallized mango. The peppercorns should be freshly ground. I have done this recipe multiple times, with both regular black peppercorns from the local grocery store as well as with Talamanca peppercorns provided by a friend from Equador. The spicyness of peppercorns varies widely and the amounts given below should be only taken as a rough guideline.

Instructions

  1. Mash at 148F for 60 min
  2. Mash at 173F for 30 min
  3. Boil for 60 min, adding hops according to the schedule on the ingredients list
  4. Cool
  5. Pitch the yeast
  6. Add secondary ingredients after initial fermentation has subsided
